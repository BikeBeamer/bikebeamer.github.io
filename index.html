Features

Beautiful images and animations

BikeBeamer produces beautiful full-color images and animations right in your bike spokes. Capable of showing over 16.9 million colors in its 70 cm wide circular canvas, it's sure to look stunning with everything it displays.

Unprecented visibility everywhere

256 high power LEDs placed across both sides of its 4 blades, each with a luminous intensity of over 2200 mcd, allow for unprecented visibility everywhere. Once the sun starts to set, BikeBeamer starts to shine and aims to keep you safe.

Easily customizable on the go

Based on the popular ESP32 development platform it is easily customizable on the go via a simple to use and straightforward web interface. Access it from any modern device with a web browser and start using your own designs - no software installation needed.

Designed to be hacked and extended

Taking customization even further, BikeBeamer is fully open source and designed to be hacked and extended. All the CAD and code is available allowing anyone to experiment with and improve upon it - all to make it truely your own.
